Heritage Landmark — The Brent Terraces

17 Roslyn Gardens is one of eight grand Victorian Italianate terraces known as the Brent Terraces (Nos. 13A–27), built c.1890. Spanning five levels with 5 bedrooms and 4 bathrooms, the property is very well maintained and retains many period features alongside some of the characterful quirks of its heritage layout. The landmark row features ornate cast-iron lacework balconies, and the property includes a carport. It sits within the Elizabeth Bay / Rushcutters Bay Heritage Conservation Area, one of Sydney's most architecturally significant inner-city precincts.
