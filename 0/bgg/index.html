Day 5 — Dunedin
Feb 19 (Thu) · Port Chalmers, NZ
Port8:15am–5:30pm

First NZ port. Scottish heritage city — Larnach Castle, Otago Peninsula wildlife (penguins, albatross, seals), Taieri Gorge Railway. Port Chalmers is 20min from Dunedin centre. Baldwin Street (world's steepest), Botanic Gardens.

Day 6 — Christchurch
Feb 20 (Fri) · Lyttelton, NZ
Port8:45am–5:30pm

Rebuilt after 2011 earthquake. Christchurch Gondola, Punting on the Avon, Canterbury Museum (free), Botanic Gardens. Akaroa day trip for Hector's dolphins (world's rarest). Lyttelton itself is arty with great cafés.

Feb 21 (Sat) · Wellington, NZ
Port7:45am–4:30pmLOTR Tour + Māori Lunch

Day 8 — Napier
Feb 22 (Sun) · Napier, NZ
Port8:45am–4:00pm

Art Deco capital — rebuilt after 1931 earthquake. Self-guided Art Deco walk (free, grab map from Art Deco Trust). Cape Kidnappers gannet colony. Hawke's Bay wineries. National Aquarium has kiwi + penguins.
